Common Appliance Issues in San Jose Homes

Here are some of the most common issues homeowners in San Jose face with their appliances:

  • Refrigerator Not Cooling: Common causes include compressor failure, thermostat issues, or blocked condenser coils.
  • Dishwasher Leaks or Fails to Drain: A clogged drain hose or faulty pump can cause these problems.
  • Washing Machine Not Spinning or Draining: Issues with the pump, hose, or drive belt could be the culprit.
  • Dryer Not Heating: A malfunctioning heating element or thermostat can cause this issue.
  • Oven or Stove Not Heating: Faulty igniters, heating elements, or control boards are common causes.
